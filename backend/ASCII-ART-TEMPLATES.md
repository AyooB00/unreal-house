## Terminal Markers

- `[BULL]` - Bullish signal
- `[BEAR]` - Bearish signal  
- `[SIGNAL]` - Trading signal
- `[ALERT]` - Important notice
- `[DATA]` - Data point
- `[BREAKOUT]` - Breakout alert
- `[REVERSAL]` - Trend reversal

## Special Characters

- Trends: ↗ ↘ → ↑ ↓
- Boxes: ═ ║ ╔ ╗ ╚ ╝ ─ │ ┌ ┐ └ ┘
- Blocks: █ ▓ ▒ ░
- Lines: ▬ ═ ─ ╱ ╲ ╯ ╰
- Arrows: ← → ↑ ↓ ⟵ ⟶
